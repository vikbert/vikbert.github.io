About Me

Hi, I'm Vikbert. This cybername comes from the names of my two kids: Viktoria & Albert.

I work as a software engineer in Berlin and enjoy my fullstack job at Schwarz Gruppe. Mostly I use the the Symfony framework (connect me) for the web projects at work and love the symfony components and its community. I am interested in "Domain Driven Design" and become an absolute believer of both Clean code and Craftsman Manifesto .

I am EXIN certificated Scrum Master and Product Owner and live in Agile software development at daily work.

In my spare time, I just jump out of the world of Object-orientation and do some lightweight coding in open-source world with both React and Svelte.
